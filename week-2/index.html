The Jolly Rhyme

Roses are red, violets are blue,

Why is the computer cold?

It left its Windows open too!

A cat in a hat, a mouse with a flute,

Silly adventures, a whimsical pursuit!

  • Wears sunglasses at night, just in case the moon is too bright
  • Dances like nobody's watching, even when everyone is
  • Sleeps in superhero pajamas, ready for dreamtime adventures
