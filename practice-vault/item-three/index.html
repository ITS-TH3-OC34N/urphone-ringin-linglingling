The Misunderstanding Of Saltybeard
Captain Saltybeard and his crew set sail on The Soggy Seagull in search of the Lost Sea treasure. A playful pod of dolphins stole their compass, leaving them lost. Relying on Saltybeard's dubious navigation, they stumbled into a giant inflatable sea monster from a beach party. Though they found no treasure, they returned with tales of being outwitted by dolphins and a rubber Kraken.
